Pumpkin and Mushroom Quiche
![Alt text](img/20241023_203920.jpg "a title")

### Ingredients for 2 persons:  
- Puff pastry: 1 sheet/package  
- Pumpkin: 600 grams  
- Cardoncelli mushrooms: 4 large ones  
- Grated Parmesan cheese: 2 tablespoons  
- Milk: 50 milliliters  
- Egg: 1  
- Extra virgin olive oil  
- Salt  
- Pepper  
- Thyme  

### Preparation:  
1. Dice the pumpkin and cook it in the microwave for 18 minutes or boil it on the stovetop until tender.  
![Alt text](img/20241023_191440.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_192018.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_192605.jpg "a title")
2. Clean and slice the mushrooms, then sauté them in a pan over low heat with olive oil, thyme, and a pinch of salt for 20 minutes.  
![Alt text](img/20241023_192028.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_192729.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_194923.jpg "a title")
3. Drain the cooked pumpkin thoroughly.  
![Alt text](img/20241023_194801.jpg "a title")
4. Using an immersion blender, blend the pumpkin with Parmesan cheese, egg, milk, salt, and pepper until smooth.  
![Alt text](img/20241023_195049.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195129.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195150.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195206.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195228.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195238.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195245.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195247.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195401.jpg "a title")
5. Combine the sautéed mushrooms (including their juices) with the pumpkin mixture and stir until well mixed.  
![Alt text](img/20241023_195429.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195432.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195459.jpg "a title")
6. Roll out the puff pastry and place it in a baking pan, shaping it to fit.  
![Alt text](img/20241023_193137.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195620.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195631.jpg "a title")
7. Pour the pumpkin and mushroom filling into the pastry shell.  
![Alt text](img/20241023_195645.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195710.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195749.jpg "a title")
![Alt text](img/20241023_195833.jpg "a title")
8. Bake in a preheated static oven at 180°C (356°F) for 30 minutes, or until the quiche is set and golden.  
![Alt text](img/20241023_200214.jpg "a title")
